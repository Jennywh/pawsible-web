Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Anonymization

Pawsible is designed with your privacy in mind. To ensure that your personal information is protected, we anonymize all data collected. This means that any personal identifiers, such as your name or contact details, are not linked to your activity or behavior within the app. Additionally, we blur precise location data, so your specific location is never disclosed.

We only collect the information necessary to provide our services effectively and enhance your experience within the app. Any data that is not required is not stored or processed.

Data Usage

The anonymized data we collect is used to improve our app's functionality, enhance user experience, and ensure the safety and security of our users. This includes:

  • Providing and maintaining services: Ensuring the app runs smoothly and efficiently.
  • Improving user experience: Analyzing app usage patterns to optimize app features and personalize content where appropriate.
  • Maintaining security: Using anonymized data to prevent fraud, ensure data integrity, and protect user information.

Data Retention and Deletion

Pawsible only retains your data for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. If you choose to delete your account, we will permanently remove all data associated with your account from our servers, ensuring that no personally identifiable or anonymized data remains.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared with trusted partners who assist us in providing essential app functionalities or services, and only under strict confidentiality agreements. All data shared with such partners is anonymized, ensuring that no personally identifiable information is disclosed.

Security of Your Information

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Although we take all reasonable steps to safeguard your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights

As a user of Pawsible, you have the right to:

  • Access and update your information: You can review and update your personal data at any time within the app settings.
  • Delete your account and associated data: Upon your request, we will remove all personal and anonymized data from our systems.
  • Contact us with questions: If you have any concerns or requests regarding your data, you can reach out to our support team.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page, and where appropriate, we will notify you via email or within the app. Your continued use of the app following these changes indicates your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
